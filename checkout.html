Common questions

Do I need a credit card to start the trial?
No. You get 14 days of full access with no card required. You'll only be asked for payment details at the end of your trial if you choose to continue.
Can I switch plans?
Yes — upgrade or downgrade any time from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately and are prorated to the day.
What happens if I cancel?
You keep access until the end of your billing period. After that, your account is paused — your data is kept for 90 days so you can reactivate any time.
Is there an annual discount?
Yes — pay annually and get 2 months free (effectively a 17% discount). Email us after signing up and we'll switch you to annual billing.
